Abstract

This page demonstrates speech enhancement results obtained using MossFormerGAN followed by an optimized mastering stage. The goal of this experiment is to evaluate how speech enhancement performs in real acoustic environments recorded across multiple meeting rooms. A clean speech reference was played and recorded in 75 different rooms. Each recording was processed using:
  • MossFormerGAN speech enhancement
  • Automatic mastering optimized via NISQA MOS prediction
Objective metrics including MOS and NISQA sub-metrics were computed for each recording.
